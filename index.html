Looking into my writing process...

Notebooks

Capture ideas as you experience them piecemeal throughout the day. Wherever you are, it is.

750 Words

Rough draft and initial exploration. Also, you’ll understand your writing and yourself via analytics.

Typora

Distill some of those concepts with some strength and certainty.

Flowstate

Take a prompt and stretch things out a bit. Discover any questions you forgot to pose initially.

GitHub

Commit writing that’s answered some questions with a reasonable of certainty.

Publish

Put the writing out there in a way that other people can experience and react to it.
